Supporting Local Organic Farming

About Us

Cedar Hollow Organics is a fourth‑generation family farm rooted in the rolling fields of Polk County. Over the years, we’ve transitioned from traditional farming to a USDA‑certified organic operation dedicated to growing nutrient‑dense produce using eco‑friendly methods. We believe healthy soil grows healthy food, and healthy food builds strong communities.

Farm Events & Education

Our farm is more than a place where vegetables grow — it’s a learning space for the entire community. From seasonal planting workshops to hands‑on sustainability classes, we welcome visitors to experience firsthand what responsible agriculture looks like.

CSA Program

Join our Community Supported Agriculture (CSA) program and receive weekly seasonal boxes filled with freshly harvested, organic produce. Being part of our CSA means becoming part of our extended farm family.

Weekly Harvest Box
