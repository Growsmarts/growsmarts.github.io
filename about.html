About Our Farm

Cedar Hollow Organics began more than a century ago when the Cedar family first cultivated this land. What started as a small homestead has grown into a thriving organic farm shaped by hard work, innovation, and a deep commitment to the environment. Today, Chrissy and Chad Cedar continue the legacy as fourth‑generation farmers, dedicating their lives to organic, regenerative practices.

We focus on sustainable agriculture — nurturing healthy soil, preserving biodiversity, and using natural growing methods. Our produce is grown without synthetic pesticides, herbicides, or GMOs, ensuring the healthiest options for families who care about what they eat.

Our mission extends beyond farming: we offer tours, workshops, and community education events that teach the next generation about sustainability, nutrition, and the importance of local food systems.
