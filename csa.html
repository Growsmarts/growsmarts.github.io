Community Supported Agriculture (CSA)

Our Community Supported Agriculture (CSA) program lies at the heart of our farm. Members receive a weekly box filled with freshly harvested, organic produce grown right here at Cedar Hollow. Each share reflects the changing seasons — from spring greens and herbs to summer tomatoes and fall root vegetables.

When you join our CSA, you’re supporting sustainable agriculture and helping us plan our crops with confidence. Your membership also connects you directly to your food source, giving you transparency, freshness, and flavor you won’t find in a grocery store.
